### TrackBetter Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 2026

TrackBetter ("the app") is developed and operated by Zain (contact: itszainla@gmail.com).

#### What data the app stores on your device

The app stores the following locally on your device:
- Expense amounts, dates, notes, and categories you enter
- Budget targets and income you set
- Payment source names (e.g. credit card labels) you add
- Recurring subscription/EMI/SIP details you add
- Financial goals you create
- Onboarding preferences

#### Where your data lives

All data is stored **locally on your device only** using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. The developer has no access to your data.

#### iCloud Sync (future feature)

A planned future update will offer optional iCloud sync. If you opt in, your data will sync across your devices using your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy policies. This feature is not available in the current beta.

#### Subscriptions

The app offers optional in-app subscriptions for premium features. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. The developer receives no payment or billing details.

#### Third-party SDKs

The app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

#### Data deletion

To delete all your data: open the app → Settings → scroll to the Data section → Reset All Data. Alternatively, delete the app from your device.

#### Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

#### Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a material way, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
